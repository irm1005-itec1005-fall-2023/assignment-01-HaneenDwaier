Our philosophy

“Yoga is not a work-out, it is a work-in. And this is the point of spiritual practice; to make us teachable; to open up our hearts and focus our awareness so that we can know what we already know and be who we already are.” — Rolf Gates

We provide a soothing space where you can explore many approaches to reaching the same outcome: creating ease in your life. We encourage you to be curious, relax, and take your time so you can leave feeling renewed.

The benefits of a regular yoga practice can include greater strength and mobility, stress-reduction, and improved posture. It reduces the risk of heart disease and joint pain, and improves mental health and clarity. It includes a sense of camaraderie and connection with other students and your favourite teachers. A regular practice can make you a happier and healthier person.
